About Us.

Whether you are buying or selling a house, ConveyancingCo is committed to making the settlement process run smoothly from start to finish.
As a team of practisicing solicitors, we bring an additional dimension to our conveyancing services from decades of experience in civil litigation.

RESIDENTIAL SETTLEMENTS.

Whether you are a buyer or vendor of residential property, ConveyancingCo can take care of settlement for you. As we deal almost exclusively with residential settlements, we have been able to streamline our processes enabling us to provide exceptional service that is affordable.

CONTRACT ADVICE.

Buying a house can be one of the biggest decisions you make. At ConveyancingCo, our solicitors can provide you with advice on the contract of sale and Form 1 so you know what you are getting into prior to signing on the dotted line (or prior to the expiry of the cooling off period).

WAIVING COOLING OFF RIGHTS.

In some circumstances, purchasers are asked to waive their cooling off rights when making an offer on a property. As qualified solicitors, we are available to provide advice and prepare the necessary documentation about waiving cooling off rights promptly, as these matters are usually very time sensitive.
